What Vemonette is

Vemonette builds a personal verification bot for large Discord servers. Members verify in one click, and your server stays protected from raids and self-bots.

Layered on top is an optional way to earn: during verification the bot can show a short sponsor line, and the traffic that creates is paid back to you. Nothing else about how your server runs has to change.

Questions partners usually ask

Will this bother my members?

No. It's the same one-click verification, with one short sponsor line before access. No forced redirects, no spam, no extra steps beyond what verification already needs.

How are clicks counted?

Only genuine, completed verifications where the sponsor line was actually shown. Every payout comes with a completion-time breakdown, so the numbers are easy to sanity-check.

When and how am I paid?

Automatically. The moment your balance reaches $10, a payout request is created and sent to your details — card, crypto, or wallet.

What do I actually have to do?

After setup, post the verification card once. The bot handles verification, sponsors, counting and payouts on its own.

Do I have to monetize?

No. The bot works purely as verification too. Earning is a switch you turn on whenever you like — and off again if you change your mind.

Is my server big enough?

It scales with real joins, so active communities benefit most — but there's no hard minimum. If people are verifying, it's working.
