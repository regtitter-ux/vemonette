What Vemonette is

Vemonette builds a personal verification bot for large Discord servers: members verify in two clicks, and you get paid for ad views.

How it works

Three steps — then it runs itself.

1

Your bot is set up

We build and host a verification bot styled for your server. Free, about 10 minutes, nothing to maintain.

2

Members verify as usual

One click, a short sponsor message, one more click. The same flow they already expect — no extra hoops.

3

You earn, automatically

$1 per 100 verified clicks. At $10 the bot files a payout to your details — you don't lift a finger.

Questions partners usually ask

Will this bother my members?

No. It's the same standard two-click verification, but with one short sponsor line before access. No forced redirects and no extra steps for the user.

How are clicks counted?

Only completed verifications where the sponsor line was actually shown.

When and how am I paid?

Automatically. The moment your balance reaches $10, a payout request is created and sent to your details — card, crypto, or wallet.

What do I actually have to do?

After setup, post the verification card once. The bot handles verification, sponsors, counting and payouts on its own.
