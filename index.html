What Vemoni is

Vemoni builds a personal verification bot for large Discord servers: members verify in two clicks, and you get paid for ad views.

How it works

Three steps — then it runs itself.

1

Your bot is set up

We build and host a verification bot styled for your server. Free, about 10 minutes, nothing to maintain.

2

Members verify as usual

One click, a jump to the sponsor's server, one more click — and they get the verified role.

3

You earn, automatically

$5 per 100 verified joins. At $10 the bot files a payout to your details — you don't lift a finger.

Questions partners usually ask

Will this bother my members?

No. It's the familiar two-click verification, with a native ad integration inside the check.

How are clicks counted?

Only fully completed verifications count — where the user saw the ad and received the verified role.

When and how am I paid?

Automatically. The moment your balance reaches $10, a payout request is created and sent to your details.

What do I actually have to do?

After adding the bot, use the /verify (role) command. The bot handles verification, sponsors, counting and payouts on its own.
