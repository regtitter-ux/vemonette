Under the hood

Two clicks. That's the whole flow.

Whatever you use it for, the member's experience is the same — fast, familiar, native to Discord.

1

Click the card

The member opens your verification card and taps the button — an ephemeral message only they can see appears.

2

Join & confirm

They join the sponsored server shown inside, then click once more. Membership is checked against Discord itself.

3

Role granted

The verification role lands instantly — unlocking channels, marking them verified, or whatever you set it to do.

Set it up in minutes

Add the bot, create a verification card with /verify, point it at a role. Done — and it can pay you back.
